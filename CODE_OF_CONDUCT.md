# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in DevPath a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of experience level, gender, age, or background.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language  
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints  
- Giving constructive feedback  
- Helping others learn and grow  

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment or offensive comments  
- Personal attacks  
- Discrimination of any kind  
- Spam or irrelevant contributions  

---

## Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for:

- Reviewing contributions fairly  
- Maintaining a respectful environment  
- Addressing inappropriate behavior  

---

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

- Report it to the maintainers  
- Provide relevant details  

All complaints will be reviewed and handled appropriately.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- GitHub repository  
- Issues and discussions  
- Pull requests  

---

## Consequences

Unacceptable behavior may result in:

- Warning  
- Temporary ban  
- Permanent removal from the project  

---

## Acknowledgement

This Code of Conduct is inspired by common open-source community standards.
